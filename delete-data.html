How to delete your data

App: Patterna · Developer: Patterna · Last updated: May 29, 2026

Patterna gives you full control over your personal data. You can request the deletion of specific data items or of your entire account at any time, free of charge, in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Option 1 — Delete data directly in the app

You can delete the following data items individually, without deleting your account, from inside the Patterna app:

  • Favourites (patterns, SBS guides, inspirations) — tap the heart icon to un-favourite
  • Community SBS guides you created — open the SBS, tap "Delete"
  • Personal notes on SBS steps — open the note, clear the text, save
  • Pattern reviews and ratings you wrote — tap "Delete" on your own review
  • Completed projects journal — tap "Delete" on a project
  • Fabric and notions inventory items — tap "Delete" on an item
  • Shopping list items — tap "Delete" on an item
  • Profile photo, name and bio — Settings → Edit profile → clear the fields

Option 2 — Delete your entire account

To delete your Patterna account and all associated personal data:

  1. Open the Patterna app and sign in
  2. Go to Settings → My data → Delete my account
  3. Confirm the deletion (irreversible)

All data is erased within 30 days of your request, except for billing records that we are legally required to retain for accounting purposes (10 years for invoices under French law).

Option 3 — Email request

If you cannot access the app, you can email us at any time. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm the deletion by email.

patterna.pro@gmail.com

Data we delete vs data we keep

Deleted on request: email, username, hashed password, Google ID, profile picture and bio, language preferences, all user-created content (SBS guides, comments, reviews, ratings, photos of finished projects, inventory, shopping list), activity data (favourites, daily streak, financial savings), and Premium subscription status.

Kept for legal compliance: billing records linked to Premium subscriptions are retained for 10 years (French accounting law). Anonymised crash logs are retained for 90 days. These records contain no directly identifying personal information after deletion of the account.

Your GDPR rights

Beyond the right to erasure, you also have the right to access, rectify, restrict, port, and object to the processing of your personal data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details, or contact us at patterna.pro@gmail.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the French data protection authority: CNIL (www.cnil.fr).
